Quark: Real-time, High-resolution, and General Neural View Synthesis

Overview

In this supplementary material, we provide comprehensive comparisons with baseline methods and analyze various design choices. At the top of this page are motion paths reconstructed and rendered at 30 fps with Quark+ for Mip-NeRF360, Tanks&Temples, and DL3DV scenes. Next we show video comparisons against the baselines, followed by image comparisons on multiple datasets, including the Real Forward Facing (RFF) Dataset, Shiny, Neural 3D Video, and DL3DV. Then, we ablate our design choices and present the corresponding image comparisons. Finally, we offer a video comparison between the real-time and high-quality versions of our method, Quark and Quark+.
